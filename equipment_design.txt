*Weapon*
Chisel - Break a freeze
Sword of Regicide - Use it to kill all enemy Queens on the board (enemy will promote to rook instead)
Hatchet - Break a shield
Mace - Break the stone of a castle (turning a rook into a pawn)
Crossbow - Fire straight down the file of the player's king, killing any pawn it strikes
Sling - Pawns sling a stone down its rank and kill the enemy queen if nothing is in the way

*Magic*
Wand of Stupdity - Reduce the AI to a level lower
Crystal Staff of Transmutation - Transform pawns into random pieces (N, B, R)

*Navigation*
Torch - Removes all Pawns of Darkness
Compass - Any checkmate move is highlighted
Equipment Key - Unlock the use of power-ups

*Clothing Accessories*
Boots - All forked non-King pieces retreat to a random open adjacent square
